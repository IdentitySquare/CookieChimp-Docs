<Accordion title="Does CookieChimp support Google Consent Mode v2?">
  Yes. CookieChimp pushes the correct `gtag('consent', ...)` calls automatically, including the `ad_user_data` and `ad_personalization` signals required by Consent Mode v2. See [Google Consent Mode](/integrations/google-consent-mode) for the full setup.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Will CookieChimp affect my site's performance?">
  No. The CookieChimp script is under 30KB gzipped, loads asynchronously and is served from a global CDN. It doesn't block rendering and won't meaningfully affect your Lighthouse scores.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I customize the cookie banner to match my brand?">
  Yes. The banner is fully themable from the [Banner Designer](/features/banner-designer) — colors, typography, corner radius, button styles and copy can all be customized.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is CookieChimp free?">
  CookieChimp has a free tier suitable for low-traffic sites, with paid plans for higher traffic and additional features. See [pricing](https://cookiechimp.com/pricing).
</Accordion>
