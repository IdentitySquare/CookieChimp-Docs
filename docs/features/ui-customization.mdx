Customize the look and feel of your CookieChimp consent banner and preferences modal directly from the dashboard. Adjust colors, text, and alignment to match your website's design.

## Customize via the Dashboard

- **Colors**: Choose colors for the banner, buttons, and text to align with your brand.
- **Text**: Update the consent message, button labels, and other text elements.
- **Alignment Options**: Adjust the positioning and layout of the banner and modal elements for optimal integration with your site.

## Advanced Customizations

For more detailed styling options, refer to the [Custom CSS](/docs/advanced/custom-css) documentation page.
