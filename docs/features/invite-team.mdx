Invite your team members to CookieChimp to streamline your organization's consent management process.
Team members can access consent records, ensuring informed decision-making.

1. **Access Team Management**: Navigate to the team management section in the dashboard.
2. **Send Invitations**: Enter team members' email addresses to send invitations.
3. **Assign Roles**: Select appropriate roles and permissions for each team member based on their responsibilities.
