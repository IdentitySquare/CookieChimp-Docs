---
title: "Webflow"
description: "Add CookieChimp to your Webflow project"
icon: "webflow"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Custom Code settings">
    In your Webflow dashboard navigate to <strong>Site Settings \u2192 Custom Code</strong>.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the CookieChimp snippet">
    Paste your website's CookieChimp JS snippet into the <code>Head Code</code> section.
    <Info>
      The script must be placed before any other scripts so that tracking scripts run only after consent.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish your site">
    Publish the changes so the snippet loads on your live site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you need to block third party scripts until consent is given, follow our [Scripts Management guide](/block-scripts-cookies/script-attributes).
</Note>
